Lia's Anime Art and Captions Contest

By Karina Torres and Mr M. | html by Yariel

Lianexis Torres started drawing anime art a few weeks ago, using Ibis Paint X on her phone. She doesn't post it anywhere, but she does share it with her friends. Everyone agrees that it is very, very good. Lia thinks she might become a professional artist and sell her drawings one day.

How did she start doing this? Sitting around, feeling connected to someone, and wanting to express that in art. "I had an idea to draw my friend and I as his favorite anime characters, and it just continued on from there."

So far Lia has made four of these drawings. To see what other students feel when they see them, The Lyceum Newspaper team created a Captions Contest, and invited all students to enter captions for the drawing they wished.

We had 8 contestants submit their entries, and then rated each of the captions from 1-5. 1 means we wished we could un-see the caption, and 5 means the caption added a lot to our appreciation of the drawing. We then added up all of our ratings together, and talked about them. It was fun!
