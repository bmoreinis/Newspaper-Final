Memes that Might Make You Laugh

By Ethan and Mr. M. | HTML by Luis

Students from Ms. Easton's Creative Writing class really liked this assignment. It was like a stress reliever because we got to use our creativity to complete an assignment. We printed out the memes and put them in the hall outside the classroom on the first floor, so maybe you have seen them.

The assignment was to find something from the study of Romeo and Juliet that you relate to and make a meme of it using a Meme Generator.

There are two things that make a good meme. The first is whether you understand what it is referring to (something about Romeo and Juliet or our experience studying it) . The second is whether you can relate to the meme and what it's trying to say - the image (joke) behind it.

Good memes do both of these things: they pick something about Romeo and Juliet that other students will understand, and the joke or meaning of the meme matches that understanding well.

If you know the play "Romeo and Juliet" by William Shakespeare, maybe you will find these funny!
